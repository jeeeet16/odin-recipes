This is the Egg Casserole recipe

Tasty egg casserole that mom approves! I frequently make this dish for breakfast because it's so easy to make.
This recipe can be easily doubled or tripled to feed a crowd, but you may have to bake it longer.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Seasonings like:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Garlic

How to make egg casserole?

Step 1:

Gather all the ingredients.

Step 2:

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

Step 3:

Mix eggs, cheese, bacon, red bell pepper, geen onion, milk, garlic, salt and black pepper together in a bowl until well combined; pour into the prepared baking dish.

Step 4:

Bake in the preheated oven until eggs are set for about 20-25 minutes.

Step 5:

Enjoy!
